How to create Youtube content better and faster

After analyzing hundreds of youtube channels I came to a clear conclusion: The best way to create quality content in a quick and easy way is to use Artificial Intelligence to assist during the content creation process.


In this post you are going to learn exactly how you can save time while getting better results on youtube.


How to get the best results with AI

Currently there are three types of videos on youtube, the ones that are 100% made without AI, the ones completely made by artificial intelligence and then there are the hybrid ones where the creators uses AI to assist them during their creative process.


AI is still not good enough to create great content by itself, but if it’s used in the right way it can boost a youtube channel in a considerable way. That’s why we recommend to use AI as your virtual assistant that allows you to create better content much faster.

Step 1. Generate AI Video Ideas

Jasper has a template that allows you to generate Video ideas, you only need to insert your channel's niche as the topic, and AI will Generate ideas that will engage viewers and rank well on YouTube.

Step 2. Generate AI Video Script Outline

As the second step, we need to generate a structure that we can follow throughout our video, which can be achieved through this template that creates the video script outline for you.

Step 3. Generate AI Video Titles

As the third step, it's important to use AI to generate engaging, click-worthy titles for your videos that will rank on Youtube. The Jasper template "Video Ideas" is perfect for that.

Step 4. Generate AI Video Script Hook and Introduction

To ensure high retention in your video, it's important to start well, that's why you should create a video hook and introduction that captures your viewer's attention and compel them to watch all the way through. With Jasper AI this step is simple since there is a template to generate a exactly what you need.

Step 5. Generate AI Video Description

The last step for your video is a good description that helps rank well on youtube, that is easily achieved through the template that generates Youtube video descriptions.
